The circuit was powered with 5V through USB and I used the

red, green and blue LEDs included in our kits.

Since the red and green LEDs have a 1.8V drop and they run at

20mA, I used 100 ohm resistors for both. Similarly, the blue

LED has a 3.3V drop and a 220 ohm resistor was used.
