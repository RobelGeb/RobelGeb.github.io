Schematic for my circuit!

The circuit was powered with 5V through USB and I used the red, green and blue LEDs included in our kits.

Since the red and green LEDs have a 1.8V drop and they run at 20mA, I used 100 ohm resistors for both.

Similarly, the blue LED has a 3.3V drop and a 220 ohm resistor was used.

Firmware running the lights!

The firmware used to control the lights was programmed in Arduino! I used a loop to initialize the pins

corresponding to each LED, then programmed them to quickly turn off and on individually 5 times before

finally turning all on at once, then all turning off.
